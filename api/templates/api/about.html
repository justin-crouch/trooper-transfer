About The Author

The only things you should know about me is that I served in the United States Army from June 2020 - November 2023. My first and last unit was 4th Squadron 2nd Cavalry Regiment in Rose Barracks, Germany. I was a scout (19D).

Throughout most of my career, I drove fat, slow, and useless military vehicles called Strykers. The leaders I chauffeured in my unit, in chronological order:

  • Platoon Senior Scout (E-6): 4 months
  • Platoon Sergeant (E-7): 1 year 6 months
  • Troop Executive Officer (O-2): 4 months
  • Troop Commander (O-3): 4 months
There were plenty others I drove for, but mainly during short training events.

During the times I was not driving, I was a dismount and a gunner on the Dragoon Stryker platform. For both roles, my instructors were the best within my Troop, and in the Squadron at some points.

Most of my respect goes to SSG John Paul Wendt, who earned Dragoon Top Gun two years in a row, for teaching me everything about the Dragoon weapon system; the same respect also goes towards SGT Tyler Nez and SGT Judah Wilkinson for providing the best dismount and life training I could ever obtain.

Before the Army, I was an honors student at Louisiana Tech University for two years. During the first year, I studied Cyber Engineering. Afterwards, I switched to Computer Science focusing in Cyber Security. I did not enjoy the teaching methods from my professors, so I left school with a 3.6 GPA and joined the Army.
